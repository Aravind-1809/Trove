Careers

Trove Research is young and dynamic organisation with a passion for making a difference. We are always looking for individuals who share our passion for helping drive climate change mitigation through data, research and insight.

WORKING AT TROVE

Trove is a fun place to work where staff are supported in their careers and encouraged to own their areas of work.  We support our staff with their own learning and development plans, through training courses, conferences and new on-the-job opportunities.

Trove started life during the Covid pandemic and we embrace flexible / hybrid working, especially for those with families, although most of our staff enjoy heading to our offices in central London (near Aldgate), Bengaluru and São Paulo several days per week.

Our staff come from a range of backgrounds as we believe an inclusive workplace culture enriches the quality and uniqueness of our insights and innovation.
